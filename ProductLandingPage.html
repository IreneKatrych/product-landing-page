Types of ties

Four-in-hand tie

Long necktie that goes around the neck with one end looping over the other end twice, then being pulled through the loop making a slip knot.

Ascot tie

Wide necktie worn looped over and held in place by scarf pin. The ends are cut diagonally.

Bow tie

Necktie, square-cut or with shaped ends, tied in a bow under the chin.
